How to begin

A short guide to getting the most from the next 182 pages.

Start with the Introduction. It is short, but it sets up everything that follows. Read it before Chapter 1, not after.

Then read Chapter 1. It is the honest audit. Do not skip the exercise at the end. Most readers say this single chapter changes how they see their finances.

When you are ready to act, go to the Conclusion. It contains a 30-day starter plan, day by day, that walks you through the first month of your new financial life.

Take your time. This is a book to live with, not finish. Real change takes patient years, not quick weekends.
